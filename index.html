I am a second year Ph.D. candidate at B. Thomas Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences of Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT). I am currently working as a Graduate Research Assistant under the supervision of Dr. Rui Li. Prior to Ph.D, I graduated in Electrical Engineeirng from the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Tehran, and I had the chance of being advised by Dr. Mohammad Ali Akhaee.

Generally, I am interested Bayesian deep learning and generative models. I have also a great interest in Continual Learning and developing different methods which allow deep neural networks to acquire and accumulate various knowledge and generalize those knowledge to the other tasks by finding more abstract representations. Currently, I am working on dynamically inferring neural network's architecture given the data by inferring the posterior distribution using Bayes rule. This dynamicity allows the capacity of network to grow when new knowledge is learned and dynamically adapt to any changes in data distribution.
